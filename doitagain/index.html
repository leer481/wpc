RACHEL'S TOP 5 TEAS

1. MATCHA


I just really like the taste of matcha or even green tea in general. Iced or hot matcha lattes, matcha ice cream, matcha cake with matcha cream cheese frosting is really good too. It also has some health benefits minus the added sugar you would drink or eat with it.

2. MANGO & LYCHEE WHITE TEA WITH LYCHEE POPPING BOBA


I’m kind of obsessed with lychee flavored things so that’s why this drink is my top 2. I really like the texture of popping boba and I think the lychee one tastes the best. I like to get this drink at Yaya Tea when I’m in NYC because their drinks taste good and aren’t too expensive. My friends and I used to walk to the one in Gramercy because it was the closest one to the dorms.

3. BLACK MILK TEA WITH TAPIOCA PEARLS


This drink is a classic bubble tea flavor. It is generally sweet with a lighter black tea taste. It’s like a drink you can chew & sip so it’s like an activity in itself. Almost every bubble tea shop has this flavor because it’s a pretty popular drink.

I’d say my favorite place to get this drink is at Limered Tea House in MA. Everything on their menu is pretty good (so I’ve heard), but I like their assam (black tea) flavor the best.

4. ICED SWEET TEA


I’m not a fan of carbonated drinks so this tea is like my alternative to soda. Again it’s a tea with a lot of sugar. Nothing better than being caffinated on a sugar high. I just like the taste of black tea and in this case, iced with a lot of sugar. I’d say the least expensive the better, unless fancy sweet tea is your thing. I’d say Pure Leaf is my favorite bottled version of this drink, but it’s a drink that you can easily make at home without messing up too badly. Also I'm a no-lemon-in-iced-tea kind of person.

5. UNSWEETENED ICED TEA


If you haven’t noticed, I have a thing for iced drinks. While I like sweet drinks, this is an unsweetened drink I actually like. It’s a good summer drink or alternative to iced coffee for me because me and the caffiene in coffee don’t mix well. I’d say Dunkin has pretty decent unsweetened iced tea, considering it’s just a tea bag brewed in water with ice, but they can also be inconsistent with the taste somehow. Again I wouldn’t drink this with a lemon, just not a fan.
