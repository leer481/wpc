    • ICED SWEET TEA

    I’m not a fan of carbonated drinks so this tea is like my alternative to soda. Again it’s a tea with a lot of sugar. Nothing better than being caffinated on a sugar high. I just like the taste of black tea and in this case, iced with a lot of sugar. I’d say the least expensive the better, unless fancy sweet tea is your thing. I’d say Pure Leaf is my favorite bottled version of this drink, but it’s a drink that you can easily make at home without messing up too badly. Also I'm a no-lemon-in-iced-tea kind of person.
