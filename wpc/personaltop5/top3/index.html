    • BLACK MILK TEA WITH TAPIOCA PEARLS

    This drink is a classic bubble tea flavor. It is generally sweet with a lighter black tea taste. It’s like a drink you can chew & sip so it’s like an activity in itself. Almost every bubble tea shop has this flavor because it’s a pretty popular drink.

    I’d say my favorite place to get this drink is at Limered Tea House in MA. Everything on their menu is pretty good (so I’ve heard), but I like their assam (black tea) flavor the best.
