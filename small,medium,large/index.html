SMALL, MEDIUM & LARGE COOKIES IN NYC

Resize your browser to explore different sized cookies you can find in NYC.

LARGE: LEVAIN BAKERY COOKIES

Levain Bakery sells these massive cookies for $4 each. You can get these large cookies in Chocolate Chip Walnut, Two Chip Chocolate Chip, Oatmeal Raisin, Dark Chocolate Chip & Dark Chocolate Peanut Butter Chip. Levain Bakery has 5 bakeries in New York in the Upper West Side, Noho, Harlem, Upper East Side & West 74th st.

MEDIUM: MILK BAR COOKIES

You can satisfy your cookie craving with a Milk Bar cookie for $2.25 for one. The Milk Bar sells 7 different flavors of cookies being the Cornflake Chocolate Chip Marshmallow Cookie, Compost Cookie, Confetti Cookie, Chocolate Confetti Cookie, Corn Cookie, Blueberry & Cream Cookie and Gluten Free Fruity Marshmellow Cookie. They have stores all over New York including in Chelsea, Williamsburg, the Upper West Side, West Village, East Village, Nomad & Nolita.

SMALL: MAH-ZE-DAHR SHORTBREAD COOKIES

These cookies, once featured on Buzzfeed's Worth It, costs only $1 for each cookie and comes in two flavors: Chocolate Sable Cookie & Vanilla Bean Shortbread. This bakery only has one location and it is at 28 Greenwich Ave, New York, NY 10011.
